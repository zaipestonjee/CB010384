The beautiful beaches of Lanka

Sri Lanka as a small country has alot of Beaches with the beatiful sands but some beaches offer different experiences to each other when visited by a traveller.

Nilaveli Beach

This Beach is just 30 minutes away from the town of Trincomalee and is visited by a number of travellers due to its beauty and palm trees.It also has a large number of Restaurants and Resorts making it convenient for the average traveller. surfing may not be a huge activity but snorkeling is conducted at pigeon island.It is also possible for adventurers to go whale watching here too.A recommended hotel with facilities and great reviews is Nilaveli Beach Hotel

Arugam Bay

This beach is regarded as one of the most famous locations for being a surfer's paradise.The best time to visit such a beach would be from May-September when the monsoon has passed It has a wide range of restaurants and hotels for visitors to enjoy their experience.If surfing is not your activity then you can go for a 2 hour lsgoon safari where even Elephants and other wildlife can be spotted.Another exciting encoutner would be to climb Elephant Rock which is at the end of the beach . A great hotel to stay at would be Whisky point resort.

Mirissa Beach

Mirissa also has a small surfing town called Weligama which is a center for people to surf. some resorts may also offer the option to go snorkeling and enjoy the beatiful sightings of marine life. This is one of the main hubs for whale watching so be sure to check it out.Mirissa has a great assortment of hotels but our recommendation would be Beach Mirissa hotel

Unawatuna Beach

Unawatuna beach is 5km just off Galle and is best known for its corals.It is also a good beach for swimming and it is populated with many restaurants and hotels improving the experience.Surfing and snorkeling are fun activities but it is possible to also go fishing.For a pleasant experience we recommend you to stay at Thambapanni Retreat

Kalkudah Beach

This beach is located 34km north West of Batticloa .It currently doesn't have many luxury hotels or restaurants so it is possible for the visitor to have a quiet getaway.It's close to the upmarket and populated Passikudah beach but doesn't have many visitors.It's one of the few beaches to have a quiet sunbathe.however as most hotels are situated closer to Passikudah our recommend resort is Karpaha Sands
