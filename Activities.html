Activities to do in Sri Lanka

When coming to Sri Lanka it is recommended to have a trip which is more than a week to experience our island properly. Despite the fact that it is a small island there are a load of encounters to be had

Some of the main activities to do in Sri Lanka are:

Safari

Hiking

Sri Lanka has beautiful scenery all around this tropical country with breathtaking views just from hiking.There are many hiking trails in Sri Lanka for any visitor to tackle and witness great sights ranging from ruins to even Tea estates.Some recommended hiking trails to visit are:

  • Adam's Peak
  • Sinharaja forest reserve
  • Horton plains
  • Ella Rock
  • Knuckles mountain range

The most recommended taken by hikers would be Adam's Peak here.It is a 2243m climb which takes around 2-5 hours depending on your fitness.When u finish the trek to the top , there is a temple at the top which also requires you to remove your shoes.However when ascending this mountain you may see the beautiful and stunning mountaneous views from the mountain. As most people aim to be on top of the mountain for the sunrise it is recommended to start the trek at 2am to have a breathtaking view of the sunrise.

Visiting local markets

Sri Lanka has a wide range of local products ranging from spices ,local produce and even local clothing types such as sarees and sarongs. There are many local markets to visit with each selling a certain range of products.Some of these markets are:
  • Pettah market -

    This is one of the biggest markets in Sri Lanka as it sells a wide range of products such as fresh produce,clothing,spices,textile products electronics and much more

  • Galle fish market -

    This is a specific market for fish as fisherman bring their haul and sell it to the customer straight from the sea.

  • Anuradhapura town weekend market -

    This market is located in a town with major temples and religious festivities.The weekend market provides ingridients from traditional Ayurvedic medicines.

There are more markets around the country which provide high quality products and will be more cheaper than shopping in big cooperations
